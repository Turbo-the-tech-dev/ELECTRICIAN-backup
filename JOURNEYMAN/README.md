# JOURNEYMAN Resources

Welcome to the JOURNEYMAN section of the ELECTRICIAN project! This directory contains resources specifically designed for licensed electricians who have completed their apprenticeship and are working independently under supervision.

## Overview

Journeymen electricians possess the skills and knowledge to perform most electrical work independently. This section provides advanced resources to help journeymen expand their expertise, stay current with codes, and prepare for potential advancement to master electrician or contractor roles.

## Professional Development

Journeymen should focus on:
- Expanding specialty knowledge
- Developing leadership skills
- Understanding business aspects
- Mentoring apprentices
- Staying current with technology

## Code Compliance

Stay current with:
- National Electrical Code (NEC) updates
- Local amendments and requirements
- OSHA safety standards
- NFPA standards
- Manufacturer installation guides

## Career Advancement

Paths for advancement include:
- Master electrician licensing
- Specialty certifications
- Contractor licensing
- Training and education roles
- Consulting opportunities

## Safety Emphasis

As a journeyman, you're responsible for:
- Ensuring your own safety
- Protecting apprentices under your supervision
- Maintaining job site safety standards
- Identifying and mitigating hazards
- Following all safety protocols consistently
