# Interview Prompts

Welcome to the interview_prompts directory! This directory contains general interview questions and prompts for evaluating electrical professionals across various roles and experience levels.

## Overview

This directory provides a collection of interview questions and prompts that can be used to evaluate candidates for electrical positions. The prompts are organized by skill level and specialty to help interviewers assess technical knowledge, safety awareness, and professional competencies.

## Sample Questions

### Entry Level
- "Can you explain Ohm's Law and its application?"
- "What safety equipment is required when working on electrical panels?"
- "How do you determine the proper wire gauge for a circuit?"
- "What is the difference between a fuse and a circuit breaker?"

### Intermediate Level
- "How would you approach troubleshooting a circuit that keeps tripping?"
- "Explain the requirements for GFCI protection in residential applications."
- "What factors affect voltage drop in electrical circuits?"
- "Describe the proper procedure for testing electrical equipment."

### Advanced Level
- "How would you design an electrical system for a new commercial building?"
- "What considerations are important for electrical systems in hazardous locations?"
- "How do you ensure proper grounding and bonding in an electrical installation?"
- "Explain the coordination requirements for selective circuit protection."
