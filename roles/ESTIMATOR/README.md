## Estimation Process

Effective electrical estimating involves:
1. **Plan Review** - Thoroughly reviewing project plans and specifications
2. **Takeoff** - Quantifying all electrical materials and labor
3. **Pricing** - Assigning current costs to materials and labor
4. **Overhead Allocation** - Including indirect costs
5. **Profit Determination** - Setting appropriate profit margins
6. **Bid Preparation** - Preparing professional bid submissions

## Cost Components

Electrical estimates typically include:
- **Materials** - Conduit, wire, devices, panels, etc.
- **Labor** - Installation, supervision, and cleanup
- **Equipment** - Tools, lifts, temporary power, etc.
- **Overhead** - Insurance, permits, transportation
- **Profit** - Return on investment for the company
