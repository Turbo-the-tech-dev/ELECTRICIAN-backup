# CONTRACTOR Resources

Welcome to the CONTRACTOR section of the ELECTRICIAN project! This directory contains resources specifically designed for electrical contractors managing businesses and projects.

## Overview

Electrical contracting involves not only technical expertise but also business acumen, project management skills, and regulatory compliance. This section provides resources to help contractors successfully operate their businesses while maintaining high standards of electrical work.

## Subdirectories

- Business formation options (LLC, Corp, etc.)

## Best Practices

- Maintain current licensing and insurance
- Develop standardized processes
- Invest in employee training
- Build strong customer relationships
- Stay current with code changes

## Success Metrics

Track these key metrics for business success:
- Profit margins
- Project completion rates
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Employee retention
- Safety incident rates
