# FOREMAN Resources

Welcome to the FOREMAN section of the ELECTRICIAN project! This directory contains resources specifically designed for electrical foremen who supervise crews and coordinate electrical installations.

## Overview

Electrical foremen serve as the critical link between management and field workers. They combine technical expertise with leadership skills to ensure projects are completed safely, on time, and to specification. This section provides resources to help foremen excel in their supervisory roles.

Foremen typically handle:
